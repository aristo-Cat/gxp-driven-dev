## 1. Objective

Analyze in detail, via FMEA, the four high-risk functions of **Temperature Data Logger for GMP Storage Chamber** identified in [RA-INIT](../../../templates/csv/RA-INIT.md), calculate their RPN, define mitigation measures, and determine the testing rigor required in OQ/PQ. This is **Step 3** of the QRM process (GAMP 5 §M3).

---

## 3. FMEA methodology

`RPN = Occurrence (O) × Relevance (R) × Detection (D)`, each factor 1-3 → RPN 1-27. Relevance (severity) is fixed by the GMP cold-storage process and **cannot be lowered by controls** (only O and D are mitigated). Double evaluation: RPN before (₁) and after (₂) mitigation. Target RPN₂ ≤ 4; residuals > 4 require formal acceptance (§5). RPN → test rigor: 1-4 GEP / 6-9 positive / 12-27 positive+negative+stress.
